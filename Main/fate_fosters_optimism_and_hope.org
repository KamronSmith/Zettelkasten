#+title: Fate fosters optimism and hope
#+filetags: 
#+date: [2023-09-28 Thu 06:40]

Belief in [[id:F768132F-E60E-4703-9AEF-9160736AC6AF][Fate]] can build optimism and hope because it helps us believe that our lives are being led according to a larger design. In hard times, trust in a bigger picture can be a great help in [[id:A138C396-39EC-43C1-B664-0361DAD7215C][managing stress]].
